Solatube

Solatube Global Marketing Inc. (SMG) is a San Diego-based exporter of tubular skylights. But these arn't ordinary skylights. They employ patented technologies that bring more natural light in, and they come with optional kits that convert the skylight into a light fixture as the natural light fades and as a ventilator for kitchen and bathroom use. As the price of electricity increases, skylights for both home and commercial use save minet and are good for the environment
